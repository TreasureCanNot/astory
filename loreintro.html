Common energy

Common Energy is energy created by Magic Stones. Common Energy is found in the common field and all Common Energy is the same. The most efficient way of using Common Energy is if a soul uses the soul stuff to transform the Common Energy into Active Energy.

Active Energy

Active Energy is energy that can be used cause different kinds of physical phenomena. This means that Active Energy can be converted into any type of Energy, including matter. Active Energy can have many interactions with other fields which is what allows it to control other fields. An example is how Active Energy can have an electromagnetic charge, this would allow someone to use Active Energy to create electromagnetic fields and stuff like that. The characteristics of Active Energy are dependent on whoever turned the common energy into the active energy, AKA the user/wizard. Different combinations of these characteristics can make literally anything possible, and that is how I justify anything being possible. The infinite amount of possible characteristics like charges and stuff allow me to make up an explanation for how any possible spell could possible work.

Spells

You can use Active Energy directly, or you can use active energy to code a spell and then use the spell. A spell is the coding of active energy. Here is an example. Imagine there is a soul with the Kinetic Affinity (moving stuff) this soul can directly use Active Energy to move a block, or the soul can code Active Energy with the instruction "move the block" and then shoot the spell to the block, and then the block will move.

You could also create "if-then" statements in real life. For example you code a spell to "if block is touched, then move block". In "if-then" statements, the input and output of a spell have to be within the same affinity, otherwise the spell will be harder to do. For example a kinetic affinity soul will have trouble making the spell "if person thinks about trees, move block" here the kinetic affinity person will have trouble with the "if" part of the spell, meanwhile a psychological affinity person would have trouble with the "then" part of the statemant which is to move the block.
