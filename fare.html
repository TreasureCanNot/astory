Welcome to my Repository

Let me explain a little something, long ago I created a website in my computer because I was bored during History, I started writing down interesting events that happened. I stopped writing a while after graduation when I left to the US for vacations. This became

The Chronicle

(not compatible with phones)

I mean I guess it's technically possible to traverse the website with your phone, but it's not made for it, unlike the diary

Then some time passed, and a lot of stuff happened during that time. But when I started University at the Italian Hospital of Buenos Aires, I decided to start writing again, this became

The Diary

(100% phone friendly))

As the names suggest, the chronicle tries(I definately failed) to be an objective account of several interesting events. On the other hand my diary is a little bit more personal at the cost of being more subjective and thus unreliable I guess. But it's funner to write and in my opinion funner to read. I also made a lot of tiny improvements to the diary, for example the diary is phone accesible while the chronicle isn't, overall the diary is a more advanced and better built website, but the chronicle has some interesting lore.

All right, so if you want to read from the beginning, you should look at the chronicle. If you hover your cursor on the top of the page you will be presented with a control pad. This will be what you will use to go to the different pages. The main page is Events, the other pages are really unnecesary and you probably never need to got there, but they might give you some context. Here are all the pages

    Guide

  • hate.html is the first page I made when I was bored in history class. I class that I hated. I just write random stuff here when I am bored.
  • gallery.html is actually pretty cool. I was going to put every picture there but I realised that there are a lot of pictures, so I only put some pictures.

  • others.html is for a short description of every person in the events page, if you somehow appear on the events page but not in the others page, then congratulations... you are irrelevant.

  • events.html

    And finally there is the events page. It's a timeline or a chronicle that started during the quarintines and went all the way to the graduation of the class of 2024.
